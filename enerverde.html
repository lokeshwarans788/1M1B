What EnerVerde Does

Every feature built around real needs.

From a single solar panel on a rooftop to a micro-grid serving a village — EnerVerde scales with your reality.

Real-Time Energy Monitoring

Live dashboards for solar, wind, and hydro sources. See generation, consumption, and storage at a glance — no guesswork, just data.

AI Optimization Engine

Our AI predicts demand peaks, learns your usage patterns, and automatically routes energy to where it's needed most — saving up to 30%.

Carbon Footprint Tracker

See exactly how much CO₂ your community avoids every day and month. Shareable reports for NGOs, schools, and local governments.

Affordable Micro-Grid Support

How It Works

Three steps to energy control.

No engineers required. If you can plug in a router, you can run EnerVerde.

STEP 01

Connect

Plug your solar panels, wind sensors, or hydro meters into any IoT gateway. EnerVerde auto-discovers devices and starts reading instantly.

STEP 02

Monitor

Our AI reads production, consumption, battery levels, and live weather data — then builds a complete picture of your energy system in real time.

STEP 03

Optimize

The system auto-routes energy to priority loads, stores surplus in batteries, and flags waste — reducing your bills without you lifting a finger.
